# Product Team

## Members
- **Chief of Staff** (lead) — strategy, vision, 10-star thinking
- **Product Manager** — discovery, PRDs, execution specs
- **Research Analyst** — market intelligence, community signal

## Workflow

```
Research Analyst    → "What is the market doing?"
Product Manager     → "What problem are we solving?"
Chief of Staff      → "Is this the right problem? 10-star version?"
Product Manager     → "Here's the spec for engineering"
```

## Weekly Cadence

| Day | Activity | Skill |
|-----|----------|-------|
| Monday | Research brief on key topics | /last30days-nux or /x-research |
| Wednesday | Product review of in-progress features | /evaluating |
| Friday | Strategy checkpoint — are we building the right thing? | /plan-ceo-review |

## Tools

All research saved to `~/Documents/Last30Days/` and `.thoughts/research/`.
Strategy decisions documented in `.thoughts/plans/` via `/writing-plans`.
